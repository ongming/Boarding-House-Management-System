1. TONG QUAN HE THONG

Ten de tai: Boarding House Management System
Muc tieu: So hoa quy trinh quan ly nha tro theo mo hinh nhieu vai tro (Admin, Staff),
bao gom quan ly phong, hop dong, chi phi, hoa don, thanh toan, phan hoi su co.

2. NHOM CHUC NANG CUA ADMIN (CHU TRO)

A. Quan ly he thong va cau hinh

2.1. Thiet lap don gia dich vu
- Quan ly don gia dien, nuoc, xe, rac.
- Cho phep cap nhat gia moi, luu vao CSDL.
- Duoc dung boi module tinh hoa don hang thang.

2.2. Quan ly tang/phong
- Them, sua, xoa thong tin phong.
- Thuoc tinh phong: so phong, tang, dien tich, gia thue co ban, noi that, trang thai.
- Trang thai phong: trong, da thue, bao tri.

2.3. Quan ly tai khoan nhan vien
- Tao tai khoan staff (username, mat khau, ho ten, ca lam).
- Phuc vu dang nhap va phan quyen.

B. Giam sat va phe duyet

2.4. Thong ke doanh thu
- Xem doanh thu theo ky: thang/quy/nam.
- Tong hop tu hoa don va khoan boi thuong.

2.5. Phe duyet tra phong
- Xem danh sach hop dong dang cho tra phong.
- Xem tong tien coc, tien no, tien boi thuong.
- Them boi thuong neu co hu hong.
- Xac nhan trang thai phong va hinh thuc thu boi thuong.

2.6. Quan ly phan hoi/su co
- Xem danh sach phan hoi tu staff/khach thue.
- Loc theo trang thai (cho xu ly, dang sua, hoan thanh).
- Cap nhat trang thai xu ly.

2.7. Tra cuu hoa don
- Tim kiem hoa don theo phong, thang, nam, trang thai thanh toan.
- Tai lai danh sach hoa don de doi soat.

3. NHOM CHUC NANG CUA NHAN VIEN (STAFF)

A. Quan ly khach thue va hop dong

3.1. Lap hop dong moi
- Chon phong trong tu man hinh Trang chu.
- Nhap thong tin khach thue: ho ten, CCCD, SDT.
- Nhap thong tin hop dong: ngay bat dau/ket thuc, so nguoi o, tien phong, tien coc.
- Upload anh hop dong (neu co).
- Tu dong cap nhat trang thai phong sau khi tao hop dong thanh cong.

3.2. Quan ly xe theo phong
- Them thong tin xe (loai xe, bien so, phi hang thang).
- Luu va hien thi danh sach xe.

3.3. Cap nhat so nguoi o
- Cap nhat bien dong nguoi o theo phong.
- Luu lich su cap nhat.

B. Van hanh va thu phi hang thang

3.4. Chot chi so dien nuoc
- Nhap chi so cu/moi theo phong va theo thang.
- Luu du lieu doc so de tinh hoa don.

3.5. Tinh hoa don
- Tu dong tinh hoa don dua tren:
  + Gia phong.
  + So dien, nuoc tieu thu.
  + Phi xe, phi rac.
- Tao hoa don hang loat theo thang.

3.6. Xem va tra cuu hoa don
- Xem danh sach hoa don cua cac phong.
- Loc theo ma phong de tim nhanh.

3.7. Xac nhan thanh toan
- Danh dau hoa don da thu.
- Ho tro hinh thuc thu: tien mat/chuyen khoan.

C. Tuong tac van hanh

3.8. Ghi nhan su co/phan hoi
- Staff ghi nhan phan hoi hu hong tu khach.
- Gui len admin de chi dao xu ly.

4. CAC CHUC NANG DUNG CHUNG

4.1. Dang nhap va phan quyen
- Dang nhap theo role Admin/Staff.
- Dieu huong den dashboard tuong ung.

4.2. Dashboard theo vai tro
- Admin dashboard: quan tri cau hinh, phe duyet, thong ke.
- Staff dashboard: thao tac nghiep vu van hanh hang ngay.

5. QUY TRINH NGHIEP VU CHINH

5.1. Quy trinh lap hop dong
- Staff chon phong trong -> mo form hop dong -> nhap thong tin -> luu.
- He thong tao tenant + contract, cap nhat phong sang da thue.
- Giao dien cap nhat ngay danh sach, thong bao thanh cong.

5.2. Quy trinh tinh va thu tien
- Staff chot chi so -> he thong tinh hoa don thang -> staff thu tien -> danh dau da thanh toan.
- Admin co the tra cuu va doi soat hoa don/doanh thu.

5.3. Quy trinh tra phong
- Staff ghi nhan thong tin tra phong -> Admin phe duyet.
- Admin nhap boi thuong (neu co), xac nhan tra coc va trang thai phong.

6. CAU TRUC FILE (KIEN TRUC DU AN)

Mo hinh kien truc:
View -> Controller -> Service -> Repository -> Database

6.1. Cau truc package chinh
- com.example.house.config
  + JpaUtil.java
  + ViewComposition.java

- com.example.house.controller
  + auth/
  + admin/
  + staff/
  + staff/home/
  + shared/

- com.example.house.service
  + admin/
  + staff/
  + impl/admin/
  + impl/staff/

- com.example.house.repository
  + admin/feature/*Repository.java
  + staff/feature/*Repository.java
  + impl/admin/feature/*RepositoryImpl.java
  + impl/staff/feature/*RepositoryImpl.java
  + impl/Jpa* (entity-level repositories)

- com.example.house.model
  + entity/
  + dto/
  + enums/

- com.example.house.view
  + auth/
  + admin/
  + staff/
  + main/

7. CONG NGHE SU DUNG

7.1. Ngon ngu va nen tang
- Java (JDK 21+)
- Maven (quan ly dependency, build)

7.2. Giao dien
- JavaFX (controls, scene, dashboard, panels)

7.3. Persistence va ORM
- Jakarta Persistence (JPA)
- Hibernate ORM

7.4. Co so du lieu
- MySQL
- JDBC driver: mysql-connector-j

7.5. Cau hinh CSDL
- persistence.xml (persistence-unit boarding-house-pu)
- Ho tro override qua bien moi truong:
  + DB_URL
  + DB_USER
  + DB_PASSWORD
  + DB_DRIVER

- Tai lieu nay la ban tong hop chuc nang va cong nghe theo cau truc code hien tai.
- Co the tach ra cac muc phu trong bao cao: Use case, Sequence, Class diagram, Testing.
